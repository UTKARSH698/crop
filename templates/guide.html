Crop Guide

Explore detailed information about various crops to optimize your farming practices.

Rice

Description: A staple food for over half of the world's population.

Growing Conditions: Warm, wet conditions (20°C - 35°C).

Soil Type: Fertile, well-drained clay or loamy soil.

Maize

Description: A cereal grain used for food and livestock feed.

Growing Conditions: Warm climates (18°C - 27°C).

Soil Type: Well-drained, fertile soil.

Jute

Description: A vegetable fiber used for making coarse threads.

Growing Conditions: Warm, humid climate (150-250 cm rainfall).

Soil Type: Alluvial or loamy soil.

Cotton

Description: A soft, fluffy fiber used to make textiles and clothing.

Growing Conditions: Long, warm growing season (21°C - 30°C).

Soil Type: Well-drained, fertile soil with a pH of 5.5-8.5.

Coconut

Description: A tropical fruit known for its versatile uses, from food to oil production.

Growing Conditions: Warm, humid climates (20°C - 30°C).

Soil Type: Sandy, well-drained soil with high organic matter.

Papaya

Description: A tropical fruit known for its sweet taste and digestive enzymes.

Growing Conditions: Warm temperatures (21°C - 33°C) and protection from frost.

Soil Type: Well-drained, fertile soil with a pH of 6.0-6.5.

Orange

Description: A citrus fruit known for its high vitamin C content.

Growing Conditions: Subtropical climates (13°C - 37°C).

Soil Type: Well-drained, loamy soil with a pH of 6.0-7.5.

Apple

Description: A popular fruit grown in temperate regions.

Growing Conditions: Cold winter and moderate summer (7°C - 24°C).

Soil Type: Well-drained, loamy soil with a pH of 6.0-7.0.

Muskmelon

Description: A sweet, juicy fruit often eaten fresh or in desserts.

Growing Conditions: Warm climates (18°C - 30°C).

Soil Type: Well-drained, sandy loam soil with a pH of 6.0-7.0.

Watermelon

Description: A refreshing fruit with high water content, perfect for hot climates.

Growing Conditions: Warm temperatures (21°C - 35°C).

Soil Type: Sandy, well-drained soil with a pH of 6.0-7.5.

Grapes

Description: Versatile fruits used for eating, winemaking, and raisins.

Growing Conditions: Warm, dry climates (15°C - 35°C).

Soil Type: Well-drained, loamy or sandy soil with a pH of 5.5-7.0.

Mango

Description: A tropical fruit known for its sweet, juicy flavor.

Growing Conditions: Warm, frost-free climates (24°C - 27°C).

Soil Type: Well-drained, fertile soil with a pH of 5.5-7.5.

Banana

Description: A tropical fruit rich in potassium and widely consumed worldwide.

Growing Conditions: Warm, humid climates (26°C - 30°C).

Soil Type: Well-drained, fertile soil with a pH of 6.0-7.5.

Pomegranate

Description: A fruit known for its antioxidant-rich seeds.

Growing Conditions: Warm, arid climates (25°C - 35°C).

Soil Type: Well-drained, loamy soil with a pH of 5.5-7.2.

Lentil

Description: Protein-rich legumes used in soups, stews, and salads.

Growing Conditions: Cool climates (18°C - 24°C).

Soil Type: Well-drained, loamy soil with a pH of 6.0-7.0.

Blackgram

Description: A pulse crop widely used in Indian cuisine.

Growing Conditions: Warm climates (25°C - 35°C).

Soil Type: Well-drained, loamy or sandy soil with a pH of 6.5-7.5.

Mungbean

Description: A legume used in soups, salads, and sprouted forms.

Growing Conditions: Warm climates (25°C - 35°C).

Soil Type: Well-drained, sandy loam soil with a pH of 6.0-7.5.

Mothbeans

Description: Drought-resistant legumes used in traditional dishes.

Growing Conditions: Arid and semi-arid regions (25°C - 35°C).

Soil Type: Well-drained, sandy soil with a pH of 6.5-7.5.

Pigeonpeas

Description: Protein-rich legumes used in various cuisines.

Growing Conditions: Warm climates (18°C - 30°C).

Soil Type: Well-drained, loamy soil with a pH of 5.0-7.0.

Kidneybeans

Description: Popular legumes used in chili, salads, and stews.

Growing Conditions: Moderate climates (15°C - 25°C).

Soil Type: Well-drained, loamy soil with a pH of 6.0-7.5.

Chickpea

Description: Protein-rich legumes used in various dishes.

Growing Conditions: Cool, dry climates (15°C - 25°C).

Soil Type: Well-drained, loamy soil with a pH of 6.0-7.5.

Coffee

Description: A popular beverage crop grown in tropical regions.

Growing Conditions: Cool, tropical climates (15°C - 25°C).

Soil Type: Well-drained, volcanic soil with a pH of 6.0-6.5.
